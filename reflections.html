Intentional reflections

Meditation without intention drifts. A clear intention turns sitting into practice.

An intention is not a goal you grasp at — it's a direction you lean toward. Before each session, name what you're practising for: patience, clarity, kindness, letting go. Afterwards, reflect honestly on what actually happened. This loop of intention and reflection is how a habit becomes a craft.

Set an intention (before)

›

What quality do I want to cultivate in the next few minutes?

›

What am I willing to let go of, just for now?

›

How do I want to carry this session into the rest of my day?

Reflect (after)

›

Where did my attention actually go?

›

What did I notice in the body, the breath, the mood?

›

What is one small thing I learned about my mind today?
